4. Intra-Sedation Record
Quick Reference - Adverse Events & Intervention (TROOPS/SIVA):
  • Apnoea: Stimulate/Airway/BVM. A short uneventful period is acceptable in a well oxygenated patient (e.g., Propofol).
  • Laryngospasm: 100% O₂ / Jaw Thrust (Larson's) / Succinylcholine if refractory.
  • Hypotension: IV Fluids / Vasoactive drugs (e.g., Metaraminol).
  • Agitation/Emergence Reaction: Reassurance / Benzodiazepine (e.g., Midazolam).
  • Reversal Agents: Naloxone / Flumazenil should be recorded as a TROOP Intervention and requires 2 hours post-sedation monitoring.
